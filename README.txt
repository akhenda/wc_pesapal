Adds Pesapal Payment Gateway to Woocommerce.

== Description ==

This plugin enables integration with Pesapal. Pesapal provides a simple, safe and secure way for individuals and businesses to make and accept payments in East Africa. Pesapal payments work on the internet and directly on the handset. The Secure features in place protect our buyers and sellers by giving them a chance to verify the quality of goods and services before releasing payments. Pesapal is PCI/DSS  Level 1 compliant.

Pesapal partners with Banks, Mobile Network Operators and Credit Card companies to give consumers as many payment options as possible. For businesses, we offer settlement of any payments to their bank account of choice.

Pesapal offers both online payments and point of sale through Sabi which available in Kenya, Uganda and Tanzania.


== Installation ==

1. Upload `wc-pesapal.zip` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
